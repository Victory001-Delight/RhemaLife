Our Story

RHEMALIFE MINISTRY — LIVING BY THE WORD

A Documentary Story
By Adegboye Blessing Noah

Introduction

Every great move of God begins as a seed, a whisper of purpose planted in the heart of a willing vessel. So began the story of Rhemalife, a vision born out of divine inspiration and a deep desire to see men revived by the living Word of God. What started as a stirring within the heart of Adegboye Blessing Noah soon became a movement of transformation, a journey of faith, obedience, and divine direction.

The Beginning

The journey of Rhemalife traces back to 2014, a year marked by spiritual awakening and hunger for God. What began as a simple fellowship among friends soon became the cradle of a movement focused on awakening hearts, restoring purpose, and revealing Christ to a generation searching for meaning. Those early days were filled with prayer, study, and encounters that shaped the foundation of what was to come. Even without a name, the fire was already kindled, a fire that would continue to spread from one life to another.

The Polytechnic Years

At The Polytechnic, Ibadan, the vision found fertile ground. Through campus fellowships, evangelism, and discipleship sessions, God began to give form to what He had birthed in the heart of His servant. Lives were being transformed. The focus was never on crowd-building, but on people-building, raising men and women whose hearts burned for God and His truth.

Those years became a spiritual training ground, preparing Adegboye Blessing Noah and others for the greater assignment ahead.

The Revealed Name (2020)

In 2020, during a season of prayer and reflection, God gave clarity and identity to the vision, the name Rhemalife was revealed. It carried divine meaning: “Rhema” the living, revealed Word of God. “Life” the visible expression of that Word in human experience. Together, the name embodied both a message and a mandate: To bring men into the reality of God’s life through His Word.

The Breakout Season (2022)

The year 2022 marked a defining phase with the birth of the Ogbomoso Vast Army Retreats, the first major revival program under the Rhemalife banner. Held at the beginning of each year, the retreat became a sacred time of renewal, re-orientation, spiritual equipping for God’s army, and realignment with heaven’s purpose. Since then, it has remained a spiritual cornerstone, shaping vision, unity, and strength within the ministry.

Rhemalife Ministry — Living by the Word

At Rhemalife, the vision goes beyond gatherings and programs; it is about transforming lives and restoring destinies through the power of God’s Word and love. One of the deepest burdens God has placed on our hearts is to become a home of healing and hope for those the world has forgotten. We believe the true essence of ministry is life touching life, reaching the hurting, the broken, and the rejected, and giving them a reason to live again.

Fostering Lives, Building Homes

Rhemalife is committed to establishing a home for children without parents, a place of warmth, education, and godly nurturing. Every child deserves love, guidance, and a future; and we are building a haven where they can grow into all that God has destined for them.

Restoring the Broken, Healing the Addicted

Through our Freedom & Recovery Initiative, we reach out to individuals battling sexual addiction, drug abuse, and other forms of bondage, guiding them to freedom through counseling, mentorship, and the healing power of God’s Word.

Comforting the Depressed and Rejected

In a world filled with silent pain, Rhemalife provides safe spaces and counseling platforms where those struggling with trauma, depression, or rejection can find light, love, and purpose again.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of believers who know God personally, live by His Word, and manifest His life wherever they go. Rhemalife envisions a people who are spiritually revived, rooted in truth, and equipped to carry divine influence into every sphere of life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring men into the reality of God’s life through:

  • The teaching of the Word: unveiling the truth of Scripture and the power of the revealed Word (Rhema).
  • Discipleship and mentorship: nurturing believers into spiritual maturity and purpose.
  • Prayer and revival meetings: creating atmospheres for encounters and transformation.
  • Outreach and kingdom expansion: extending God’s love and influence through acts of compassion and evangelism.

Closing

To revive lives, restore hope, and raise vessels who will, in turn, bring the light of God to their generation. Rhemalife — giving life through the Word, and love through action.
